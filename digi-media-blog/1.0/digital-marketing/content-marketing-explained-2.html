Content marketing means attracting and transforming prospects into customers by creating and sharing valuable free content. The purpose of content marketing is to help companies create sustainable brand loyalty and provide valuable information to consumers, as well as create a willingness to purchase products from the company in the future.

Common metrics:

  • Brand awareness and visibility
  • Brand health metrics
  • Diversified user base
  • Sales

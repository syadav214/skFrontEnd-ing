A village in Pune has ritual to check virginity before marriage. It had been so embarrassing for women. There are a group of youngsters who to break this taboo. In process to that, they started a WhatsApp Group - Stop the V Ritual.

Groups’ mission is to make awareness to society. And privacy of human being should be maintain. Most importantly for the woman who faces a lot of embarrassments. These 3 guys are frontier and wanted to show the issues within the society.

But, they are people who don’t want to change and are against them. Few days ago, those people had beaten Stop the V Ritual admins. Police have arrested 2 person who were involved in brawl.

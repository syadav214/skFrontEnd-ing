I was travelling to Mumbai from Nagpur on 09 Dec 2017. I bought general ticket. And took Train 18030/Shalimar - Mumbai LTT Kurla Express. I got into sleeper coach. I thought about paying the fine and happy get a place to sit.

TTE came just after we left Nagpur. TTE was checking tickets of passengers. Many passengers said that they belong to RPF, Army or Police. Some of them said that their elder brother works in Indian Railways at TTE post. TTE accepted those and moved ahead. He asked me. I told him that I have general ticket. He asked me to come to away from public; near toilet. He asked 800 rupees and said he will provide a seat to me till Mumbai. I close the deal on 700 rupees.

A man sat near to me, after an hour. I asked him do you have ticket. He replied that I have a ticket which not yet confirmed (Waiting List). Then I thought it is completely unfair to that guy. I tweeted to @railminindia. I did not get proper response from them, instead of that a man named Khemdev Sharma replied on the tweet. He tried to prove I am wrong.

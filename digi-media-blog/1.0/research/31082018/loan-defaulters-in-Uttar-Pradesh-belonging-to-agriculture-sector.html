There are lots of loan defaulters in Uttar Pradesh belonging to agriculture sector.
Banks and state government should check the background and profile of a person before issuing loan to the person.

We met few villager in district of Jaunpur. Many of them are in farming and animal husbandry i.e. Tabela in Hindi. We would use the same in the article. They were laughing while talking about theirs story of getting loan waive off.
One of them said, "I got loan and bought 5 buffalos. 4 out 5 were very old and had diseases; They died and I got insurance compensation. We will get loan waive off due to drought. We are set. I got profit of 10 lakh INR without doing business."

We propose a solution to cater this problem. Which is to have digital platform to monitor the fund allocation via loan usage of it against Aadhaar or Pan card.

This shows a picture of India where from self-employed person to a business tycoons are doing wrong practice to default of loan. Hard earned taxpayers money is going in the hand of fraudster.
We believe government will come up with some solution other than provided by us.

We learned that loan defaults increased to INR 83,153 crore in a year.
